Welcome to the Common Public-Sector Catalogue of Concept and Datamodels

This site serves as an overview of the concept and data models created in the public-sector domain and that have been registered for the purpose of knowledge sharing and reuse. Furthermore this catalogue contains information about a number of internationally recognized models, that can be explored and applied or profiled for use in the Danish administrative and public context. The use of the Common Public-Sector Rules for Concept and Data Modelling and reuse of models and data enables us to create a common public-sector digital architecture and a linked data framework where we can reuse models and the data they document.
